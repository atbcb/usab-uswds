David O’Keefe serves as the Communications Specialist in the Office of Executive Director at the Access Board. In this role, he manages the Board’s communication channels, which include social media, news releases, and website content. Additionally, his work involves planning and coordinating logistics for virtual and in-person public events, such as the agency’s quarterly Board meetings. He previously served as a Team Leader for FEMA Corps (AmeriCorps NCCC) and as the Cultural Affairs Assistant for the State University of New York (SUNY) at Orange County Community College. O’Keefe is a Certified Technical Writer (CTW) and holds an M.A. in Technical and Professional Writing from Middle Georgia State University and a B.A. in Digital Media Production from SUNY New Paltz.
