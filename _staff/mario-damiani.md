Mario Damiani, Senior Compliance Specialist, has worked at the Access Board since 2014. Damiani handles complaints filed with the Board alleging violations of the Architectural Barriers Act (ABA) of 1968. This work involves making legal determinations regarding jurisdiction and the applicable ABA standards and, where violations of the standards are determined to exist, working with agencies to ensure that violations are remediated through corrective action.
