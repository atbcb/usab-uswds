Grab your sunglasses and sunscreen and let’s take a day trip to the beach! Our day will start with plenty of time on the sand but will include a stop at a favorite boardwalk store and restaurant, and finish with time on the amusement rides. This day trip will be filled with visual imagery along with an overview of the applicable Standards from the Architectural Barriers Act (ABA) and Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) Accessibility Standards. Actual technical assistance scenarios will be used to provide real-life examples and to further knowledge of applying the Standards. Registrants will be able to submit questions in advance during the registration process. Real-Time Captioning and Sign Language Interpretation will be provided.
