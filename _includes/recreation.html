Access to recreation facilities, including play areas, swimming pools, sports facilities, fishing piers, boating facilities, golf courses, and amusement rides is addressed in the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and Architectural Barriers Act (ABA) Standards.  Provisions cover access to trails, picnic and camping sites, and beaches on Federal sties.
