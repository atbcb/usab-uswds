The U.S. Access Board is seeking a Management and Program Analyst, GS-0343-14. A successful candidate will lead and execute projects and studies on key issues, program goals and objectives, work processes, and administrative operations of the agency. The Management and Program Analyst will prepare project papers, staff reports, and briefings for deliberation and decision-making purposes by senior leadership and encourage understanding and acceptance of findings and recommendations.

This position is open to the public and government-wide and closes on April 20, 2026.

To view the public announcement, 26-AB-12928100-P,  please click the following link: [https://www.usajobs.gov/job/864536100](https://www.usajobs.gov/job/864536100).

To view the government-wide announcement, 26-AB-12928101-MP,  please click the following link: [https://www.usajobs.gov/job/864535100](https://www.usajobs.gov/job/864535100).

For further information, contact the Applicant Call Center at (304) 480-7300 or by email at [accessboardinquiries@fiscal.treasury.gov](mailto:accessboardinquiries@fiscal.treasury.gov).
