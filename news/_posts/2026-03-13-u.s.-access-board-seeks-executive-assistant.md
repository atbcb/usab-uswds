The U.S. Access Board is seeking an Executive Assistant GS-0301-11.  A successful candidate will provide high level executive administrative support and provide a wide variety of technical and non-technical work to fully support the Executive Director. The Executive Assistant acts in a confidential capacity and performs specialized duties with responsibility for processes, projects, questions, and problems within the Office of the Executive Director.

This position is open to the public and government-wide and closes on March 18, 2026.

To view the public announcement, 26-AB-12903905-P,  please click the following link: [https://www.usajobs.gov/job/860755700](https://www.usajobs.gov/job/860755700).

To view the government-wide announcement, 26-AB-12903906-MP,  please click the following link: [https://www.usajobs.gov/job/860755300](https://www.usajobs.gov/job/860755300).

For further information, contact the Applicant Call Center at (304) 480-7300 or by email at [accessboardinquiries@fiscal.treasury.gov](mailto:accessboardinquiries@fiscal.treasury.gov).
