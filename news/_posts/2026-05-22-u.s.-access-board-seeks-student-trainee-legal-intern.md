The U.S. Access Board is seeking a Student Trainee (Legal Intern), GS-0999-7/9. A successful candidate will perform research and prepare necessary documentation as requested by attorneys in the course of litigation, investigations, and other legal work.

This position is open to the public and closes on June 04, 2026.

To view the public announcement, 26-AB-12955125-X, please click the following link: [https://www.usajobs.gov/GetJob/ViewDetails/870366700](https://www.usajobs.gov/GetJob/ViewDetails/870366700).

For further information, contact the Applicant Call Center at (304) 480-7300 or by email at [accessboardinquiries@fiscal.treasury.gov](mailto:accessboardinquiries@fiscal.treasury.gov).
